ABOUT US

8 Years Strong
Our Story

BUILT WITH PASSION, DRIVEN BY RESULTS

Iron Pulse Gym was founded in 2017 with a single mission: to create a space where everyone — from first-timers to seasoned athletes — could push beyond their limits and discover their true potential.

What started as a 2,000 sq ft facility with 3 trainers has grown into a 12,000 sq ft premium fitness campus with 15 certified professionals, 200+ pieces of equipment, and a community of over 850 active members who inspire each other every single day.

  • 12,000 sq ft state-of-the-art facility
  • 200+ premium machines & free weights
  • Internationally certified trainers
  • Dedicated cardio, strength & yoga zones
  • Recovery room with steam & cold plunge
